User Manual

QUIZ

Click "start" on the home page to receive 5 randomized questions. Some fill-in-the-blank questions offer hints; choosing to receive one results in a deduction of 1 point from the final score (out of 10) until the final score is zero. The submit button only functions once all questions have been answered. A printable quiz report will be available after completion that displays the scores as well as the correct answers to the quiz.

Watch out for spellings!

CHANGE ACCOUNT INFO

Username and password can be changed via Advanced Settings > Account

EDIT QUESTION BANK

A registered account comes with 60 default questions. Through Advanced Settings > Question Bank, questions may be added, edited, or deleted. Beware: deleted questions cannot be retrieved. Questions can be deleted individually or in bulk. The entire question bank can also be reset with default questions only or with no questions. The save button must be clicked in order for changes to be saved.
